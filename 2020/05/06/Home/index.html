About me

Welcome to my home page!

I am Jia-Qi Zhang, a student with the State Key Laboratory of Virtual Reality Technology and Systems, Beihang University, supervised by Prof. Shi-Min Hu and Prof. Miao Wang. I obtained the BS degree and master’s degree in software engineering from North China Electric Power University, supervised by Prof. Su-Qin Wang. My current research interests include virtual reality, computer vision and computer graphics.
